Before you begin / Boshlashdan oldin:
  • Total: 30 multiple-choice (60 pts) + 2 coding problems (40 pts) = 100 points. Jami: 30 ta test (60 ball) + 2 ta kodlash masalasi (40 ball) = 100 ball.
  • You have 80 minutes. The timer appears top-right and auto-submits at 00:00. Sizga 80 daqiqa vaqt beriladi. Taymer yuqori-o'ngda ko'rinadi va 00:00 da avtomatik yuboriladi.
  • Each multiple-choice question has exactly one correct answer. Har bir test savolida aniq bitta to'g'ri javob bor.
  • Do NOT switch browser tabs or open other windows. Tab switches are logged and flagged. Brauzer yorliqlarini almashtirmang yoki boshqa oynalarni ochmang. Almashtirishlar qayd etiladi.
  • Copy-paste and right-click are disabled. Write your code yourself. Nusxa ko'chirish va o'ng-tugmacha o'chirilgan. Kodingizni o'zingiz yozing.
  • After you click Submit, a PDF will be downloaded automatically. Upload it via the Google Form shown on screen. Yuborish tugmasini bosgandan so'ng PDF avtomatik yuklab olinadi. Ekrandagi Google Form orqali yuboring.
